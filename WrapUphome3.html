Plants

Normal

These are original plant that is assume to be the starting point for all other variations of its kind, for example looking at a pea shooter, the original pea shooter can be assume to be the classic one, while the variations could be a fire peashooter or a ice peashooter, plants can also receive an power buff that increase their fire power for around seven to ten seconds, these can be use in crucial moments to turn the tide of an game around. Variation

These are plant that "evolve" from their original counter part, that is how I acknowledge plant that looks extremely similar to their original plant, for example an wall nut starts out plain, but as you continue to play the game you soon discover many more plant that act similar but different in some way when compare to the wall nut, for example theres an variation of the wall nut that blocks up all five lanes but with little protective power, while the original can only protect one lane.
