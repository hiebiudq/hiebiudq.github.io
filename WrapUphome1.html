Game Play

This game revolves around the idea of the user planting down plants to defend their home against a variety of zombies, as the user progresses throughout the game the user unlocks more plants to defend against stronger enemies such as bosses and machines. On the right side is one of the first plants that the user unlocks: A peashooter

After beating certain levels the user can gain more plants that targets toward an certain type of zombie, for example plants that target flying zombies, there are also plants that are well round toward anytype of zombie, such as the classic pea shooter that the user first unlock. On the left side is one of the first enemy the user face : An regular zombie.
