Spider-Man: No Way Home

Peter Parker asks Doctor Strange to cast a spell to make everyone forget he is Spider-Man, but the spell fractures reality and villains from other universes arrive in New York. They fight across the city as Peter learns how difficult it is to save everyone while keeping his identity secret. (Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spider-Man:_No_Way_Home)

I like this film because it blends action and emotion, serving as a send-off for earlier Spider-Men while giving Tom Holland a powerful arc. The team-ups and callbacks are nostalgic, and the stakes are huge. This content was created with the help of A.I.

  • Spider-Man (Peter Parker) - first introduced in the original 1962 comic; in this film, he learns what it means to be a hero without praise.
  • MJ (Michelle Jones) - her witty attitude and loyalty help Peter cope with the chaos of the multiverse.
  • Doctor Strange (Stephen Strange) - a master of mystic arts, he becomes the unexpected ally and source of the spell that opens the multiverse.
  • Green Goblin (Norman Osborn) - one of the most dangerous villains in Spider-Man lore, he returns with a personal vendetta and advanced tech.
