Plant Vs Zombie

Plant Vs Zombie is always an iconic game and wellknown for a lot of people, this game consist of simple gameplay and yet captivates a lot of peoples attention and overall creates an amazing experience for most people even without high detail graphics. For me I love this game as the user needs to think about their load out when dealing with different types of zombie or levels, this is very brilliant as it allows to user to not only create their own experience but also giving the user and original gameplay.
